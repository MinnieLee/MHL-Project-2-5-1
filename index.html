Q1.

Why did you choose the main theme as value?

A1.

At first, I was interested in what people would think is valuable in a busy society, and later I continued to think about what is valuable in my life. As I continued to think about this, I came to think that I wanted to make works based on these ideas. I wanted to express that there is no absolute value in a thing and the value of it varies depending on the relationship between the thing and people.
